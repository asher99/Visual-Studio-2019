The Patient Community Outreach, Education and Safety program engages patients and their family members to manage their healthcare. The PatientAider is a valuable resource of medical information that is designed to empower patients by explaining common medical terms, protocols in the hospital that should be implemented to keep them safe, and risks that patients may encounter. Implementing patient and family engagement strategies has led to fewer hospital-acquired infections, reduced medical errors, reduced serious safety events, and increased patient satisfaction scores.
Medical Errors are the THIRD leading cause of death in the U.S. killing over 200,000 patients annually in hospitals. Improving the delivery of healthcare, reducing medical errors, putting best practices to work, and adopting a more patient-centered approach all contribute to higher quality of care. Your gift will help ensure that patient safety is the cornerstone of high-quality health care.
